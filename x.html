About Us

Operating Hours: Mon-Sat, 08:30-17:00

Dr. Tee Hui Kien started this clinic on Jun-2000, at one of the shoplots in Jalan Bandar 1, Pusat Bandar Puchong.

After 12 years of 6-days a week hardwork, this clinic has been upgraded and moved to a 5th floor in SetiaWalk, an open-mall next door. Although it is slightly tougher to reach, but the Genting Highland's view makes the climb worth.

This clinic has been providing quality dental care to the Puchong community for more than 20 years.
